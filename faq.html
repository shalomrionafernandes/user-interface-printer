Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Priint It?

Priint It is a platform that connects users to the nearest printer shops, allowing them to upload documents, select print options, and pay in advance for a hassle-free experience.

How do I upload my documents?

You can upload your documents by clicking on the 'Upload' button on the Priint It website or app and selecting files from your device.

Can I customize my print options?

Yes, Priint It allows you to choose options like paper size, color or black-and-white printing, number of copies, and other preferences.

How do I pay for my prints?

You can pay securely using various online payment methods, including credit/debit cards, UPI, and mobile wallets.

Are delivery options available?

Yes, Priint It offers delivery options so you can receive your prints at your doorstep without visiting the shop.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you can track its status in the 'Orders' section of your Priint It account.

What if the shop is closed?

Priint It notifies you about the operational status of shops before you place an order. You can choose another shop or schedule for later.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it is processed by the shop. Refunds will be initiated as per our policy.

Is there a support team?

Yes, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you via chat or email at support@priintit.com.
